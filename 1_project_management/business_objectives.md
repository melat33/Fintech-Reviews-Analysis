# Business Objectives

## Primary Goal
Analyze customer satisfaction and sentiment across Ethiopian financial mobile applications to provide actionable insights for service improvement and competitive positioning.

## Specific Objectives

### 1. Customer Experience Analysis
- Identify pain points in mobile banking user experience
- Measure customer satisfaction levels across different banks
- Track sentiment trends over time

### 2. Competitive Intelligence
- Compare performance metrics across 5 major Ethiopian banks
- Identify strengths and weaknesses of each mobile banking platform
- Benchmark against industry standards

### 3. Service Improvement
- Pinpoint specific features needing enhancement
- Provide data-driven recommendations for UX/UI improvements
- Identify opportunities for new feature development

### 4. Strategic Decision Support
- Enable data-driven product development decisions
- Support customer retention strategies
- Inform marketing and communication strategies

## Target Banks
1. Bank of Abyssinia (BoA Mobile)
2. Commercial Bank of Ethiopia (CBE Mobile)
3. Dashen Bank (Dashen Mobile)
4. Zemen Bank (Zemen Mobile)
5. Abay Bank (Abay Mobile)

## Success Criteria
- 90%+ accuracy in sentiment classification
- Identification of 3-5 key improvement areas per bank
- Actionable recommendations for each financial institution
